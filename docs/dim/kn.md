## Brug

Simpel join:

```sql
SELECT f.indhold, d.titel
FROM fact.<fact_tabel> f
JOIN dim.kn d ON f.kn = d.kode
WHERE d.niveau = 1
```

Aggregering via parent_kode (fra fineste til groveste niveau):

```sql
SELECT top.titel, SUM(f.indhold)
FROM fact.<fact_tabel> f
  JOIN dim.kn detail ON f.kn = detail.kode AND detail.niveau = 5
  JOIN dim.kn mid ON mid.kode = detail.parent_kode AND mid.niveau = 4
  JOIN dim.kn mid2 ON mid2.kode = mid.parent_kode AND mid2.niveau = 3
  JOIN dim.kn mid3 ON mid3.kode = mid2.parent_kode AND mid3.niveau = 2
  JOIN dim.kn top ON top.kode = mid3.parent_kode AND top.niveau = 1
GROUP BY top.titel
```
