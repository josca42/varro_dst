table: fact.tema9007
description: Udledning af drivhusgasser efter enhed og tid
measure: indhold (unit Mio ton CO2 -ækvivalenter)
columns:
- enhed: values [435=Udledning fra dansk økonomi, inkl. dansk transportvirksomhed i udlandet, 438=Udledning i Danmark]
- tid: date range 1990-01-01 to 2024-01-01

notes:
- enhed has two alternative totals: 435=economy principle (Danish economic activities incl. transport abroad), 438=territorial principle (emissions in Denmark). These represent different accounting frameworks — do NOT sum across enhed.
- Unit is Mio ton CO2-equivalents. Quick overview table; for sector breakdown use drivhus (1.000 ton) which has the same economy principle.
- 435 in tema9007 corresponds to DSTTOTAL in mro2; 438 corresponds to DKTOTAL in mro2.
