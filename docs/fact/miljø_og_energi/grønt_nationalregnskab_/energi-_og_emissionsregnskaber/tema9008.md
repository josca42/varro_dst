table: fact.tema9008
description: Drivhusgasser pr.indbygger efter land og tid
measure: indhold (unit Ton CO2-ækvivalenter pr indbygger)
columns:
- land: values [350=Belgien, 386=Bulgarien, 230=Danmark, 255=Estland, 384=EU-27 ... 382=Tjekkiet, 285=Tyrkiet, 295=Tyskland, 325=Ungarn, 240=Østrig]
- tid: date range 2000-01-01 to 2020-01-01

notes:
- International comparison of GHG per capita. indhold in Ton CO2-equivalents per inhabitant. Do not sum across countries.
- Includes EU-27 aggregate (land=384) and individual EU/OECD countries plus Turkey. Runs 2000–2020 only (no recent data).
- For Denmark-only GHG trends, use drivhus or tema9007.
