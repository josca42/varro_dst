table: fact.sdg07021
description: Vedvarende energis andel af det samlede bruttoenergiforbrug efter område og tid
measure: indhold (unit Pct.)
columns:
- omr20: values [00X=I alt]
- tid: date range 1990-01-01 to 2024-01-01

notes:
- Single-series table: omr20 only has one value (00X=I alt). Just filter by tid to get the annual renewable energy share for Denmark.
- indhold is in Pct. — already a percentage. Do not sum across years. Series runs 1990–2024.
- This is a SDG indicator table (SDG 7.2.1). For the underlying energy data, see ene3h or ene2ho.
