table: fact.are207
description: Areal 1. januar efter område og tid
measure: indhold (unit Km2)
columns:
- omrade: join dim.nuts on omrade=kode; levels [1, 3]
- tid: date range 2007-01-01 to 2025-01-01
dim docs: /dim/nuts.md

notes:
- omrade joins dim.nuts at niveau 1 (5 regioner) and niveau 3 (99 kommuner) — no landsdele (niveau 2). Always filter d.niveau to avoid mixing levels and double-counting.
- omrade='0' is "Hele landet" (whole country total) — not in dim.nuts, it's an inline aggregate. To get the national total without joining, use WHERE omrade = '0'.
- Simple table with only two dimension columns (omrade, tid). No enhed/unit selector — indhold is always km2.
- Map: /geo/kommuner.parquet (niveau 3) or /geo/regioner.parquet (niveau 1) — merge on omrade=dim_kode. Exclude omrade=0.
