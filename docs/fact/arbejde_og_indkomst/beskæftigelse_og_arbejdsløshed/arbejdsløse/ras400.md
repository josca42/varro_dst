notes:
- Workforce denominator (ultimo november snapshot) for computing unemployment rates by region/age/gender. Use together with aul01/aulk01 numerator.
- kon has no TOT — must aggregate M+K manually for total.
- alder has special codes 65UP/65MP/66UP/66MP for pension-age edge cases (2019/2021 changes). These are subsets of 65-66 band; use TOT to avoid double counting.
- Stopped at 2023. Annual snapshots only (ultimo november = end of November each year).
- Map: /geo/kommuner.parquet (niveau 3), /geo/landsdele.parquet (niveau 2), or /geo/regioner.parquet (niveau 1) — merge on omrade=dim_kode. Exclude omrade=0.
