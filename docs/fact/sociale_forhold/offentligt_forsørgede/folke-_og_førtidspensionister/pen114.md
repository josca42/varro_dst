table: fact.pen114
description: Folkepensionister efter område, køn, alder, ydelsestype, modtagere og tid
measure: indhold (unit -)
columns:
- omrade: join dim.nuts on omrade=kode [approx: Missing codes: 0 (placeholder), 997 (likely Hele landet)]; levels [2, 3]
- kon: values [MOK=Mænd og kvinder i alt, M=Mænd, K=Kvinder]
- alder: values [TOT=Alder i alt, 6569=65-69 år, 7074=70-74 år, 7579=75-79 år, 8089=80-89 år, 9099=90 år og derover]
- ydelsestype: values [46=Folkepension i alt, 47=Folkepension uden pensionstillæg (brøkpension undtaget), 48=Folkepension med fuldt pensionstillæg (brøkpension undtaget), 49=Folkepension med reduceret pensionstillæg (brøkpension undtaget), 50=Brøkpension til folkepensionister]
- modtag1: values [80=Modtagere (antal), 82=Nye modtagere (månedsvis), 84=Andel af befolkningen (promille)]
- tid: date range 2021-01-01 to 2025-06-01
dim docs: /dim/nuts.md

notes:
- omrade joins dim.nuts at niveau 2 (11 landsdele) and niveau 3 (98 kommuner). There is no niveau 1 (regioner) in this table. Use ColumnValues("nuts", "titel", for_table="pen114") to see available regions. Filter WHERE d.niveau = 2 for landsdele or WHERE d.niveau = 3 for kommuner — never join without a niveau filter or you'll double-count.
- omrade=0 is "Hele landet" (national total, 1,069,604 folkepensionister in 2024). omrade=997 appears to be people living outside Denmark or with unknown address (~46,555 in 2024) — it is not in dim.nuts and should be excluded from regional breakdowns.
- modtag1 is a measurement selector: 80=antal modtagere, 82=nye modtagere (monthly flow), 84=andel af befolkningen (promille). Always filter to a single modtag1 value. Note that modtag1=82 (nye modtagere) only exists for ydelsestype=46 (Folkepension i alt) — it is not available for the sub-types (47–50).
- alder has TOT as a total plus 5 age bands (6569, 7074, 7579, 8089, 9099). TOT=sum of the 5 bands. Filter alder='TOT' for overall counts and filter to a specific band when doing age analysis.
- ydelsestype=46 (Folkepension i alt) is the total; 47–50 are sub-types. Do not sum 46 with 47–50, as 46 already includes them.
- To count total folkepensionister nationally: WHERE omrade='0' AND kon='MOK' AND alder='TOT' AND ydelsestype='46' AND modtag1='80'.
- Map: /geo/kommuner.parquet (niveau 3) or /geo/landsdele.parquet (niveau 2) — merge on omrade=dim_kode. Exclude omrade IN (0, 997).
