table: fact.ky01
description: Kontanthjælp (personer) efter visitation, alder, køn og tid
measure: indhold (unit Antal)
columns:
- visitation: values [TOT=I alt, 1=Jobparat, 2=Aktivitetsparat, 3=Åbenlyst uddannelsesparat, 4=Uddannelsesparat, 9=Uoplyst]
- alder: values [TOT=Alder i alt, 16-24=16-24 år, 25-29=25-29 år, 30-39=30-39 år, 40-49=40-49 år, 50-59=50-59 år, 6099=60 år og derover]
- kon: values [TOT=I alt, M=Mænd, K=Kvinder]
- tid: date range 2007-01-01 to 2025-06-01
notes:
- Pure person-count table (no payment amounts). Use for monthly recipients by visitation, age, and gender.
- visitation has the most detailed breakdown available: 1=Jobparat, 2=Aktivitetsparat, 3=Åbenlyst uddannelsesparat, 4=Uddannelsesparat, 9=Uoplyst. More granular than kys01.
- All three dimension columns have TOT rows. For a simple monthly headcount: WHERE visitation='TOT' AND alder='TOT' AND kon='TOT'.
- alder has 6 bands (16-24, 25-29, 30-39, 40-49, 50-59, 60+). More detail than kys01 but still grouped — no individual ages.
