table: fact.pris4621
description: Prisindeks for indenlandsk vareforsyning efter branchehovedgrupper, enhed og tid
measure: indhold (unit -)
columns:
- hovedgrp: join dim.db on hovedgrp=kode::text [approx]; levels [2, 3]
- enhed: values [100=Indeks, 200=Ændring i forhold til måneden før (pct.), 300=Ændring i forhold til samme måned året før (pct.)]
- tid: date range 2014-01-01 to 2025-09-01
dim docs: /dim/db.md

notes:
- enhed is a measurement selector that triples the data — always filter to one value: enhed='100' (Indeks level), enhed='200' (month-on-month change %), or enhed='300' (year-on-year change %).
- Do NOT use the dim.db join for hovedgrp. Same issue as pris4321: the column mixes numeric NACE codes (matching dim.db at niveau 2), letter aggregate sections (B, BC, BCDE, C, D, E), and custom S-codes (S1–S6) that are not in dim.db. The numeric codes are also ambiguous across hierarchy levels.
- Use ColumnValues("pris4621", "hovedgrp") instead — labels are already embedded in the column values parquet.
- This table is the domestic supply price index (indenlandsk vareforsyning) by branch. Unlike pris4321, there is no market split (no marked1 column) — it covers total domestic supply only. From 2014 onwards.
- For a longer series covering domestic supply by branch but without the market split: pris4621 is the right table. For market breakdowns (home vs export vs import), use pris4321.
