notes:
- amt same 4-level geography as other bol20x tables: 0=Hele landet, 81-85=Regioner, 1-11=Landsdele, 101+=Kommuner. Filter to one level to avoid overcounting.
- husstor (household size 1-7+) and antborn (children 0-5+) are not mutually exclusive in their role — persons from the same household appear under the same husstor/antborn. Summing across both dimensions is fine as long as you're counting persons, not households.
- antvaer tops at '6-' (6+ rooms). No total row — sum all antvaer values for the full count.
- No total rows in any column. SUM(indhold) grouping by one dimension gives that dimension's breakdown.
- Map: /geo/kommuner.parquet (amt 101–851), /geo/landsdele.parquet (amt 1–11), or /geo/regioner.parquet (amt 81–85) — filter amt to one level, then merge on amt=dim_kode. Exclude amt=0.
