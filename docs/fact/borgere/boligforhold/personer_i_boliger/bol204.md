notes:
- amt same 4-level geography as other bol20x tables: 0=Hele landet, 81-85=Regioner, 1-11=Landsdele, 101+=Kommuner. Filter to one level to avoid overcounting.
- hustyp has 7 values: 1M=Enlig mand, 1K=Enlig kvinde, PAR=Ægtepar, PA=Et par i øvrigt, IHB=Ikke hjemmeboende børn under 18 år, AH=Andre husstande (multi-family), FH=Flere husstande i boligen. IHB means children registered at the address but living elsewhere. No total row.
- udlforh in bol204 adds 'IB=Ikke benyttet bolig' (unoccupied) compared to bol201/203. Filter to EJ or LEJ to count people in occupied dwellings.
- No total rows in any column. This is the table for questions about household composition (singles, couples, family types).
