table: fact.hdyr1920
description: Dyrebestanden (historisk) efter husdyrtype og tid
measure: indhold (unit 1.000 stk.)
columns:
- husdyrtype: values [1035=Kvæg i alt, 1010=Tyre 1 år og derover, 1015=Stude 1 år og derover, 1020=Køer, 1025=Kvier ... 1150=Hopper i alt, 1155=Bedækkede hopper, 1160=Andre hopper, 1165=Plage, 1170=Føl]
- tid: date range 1920-01-01 to 1981-01-01

notes:
- Historical series only (1920–1981). Pairs with hdyr1 (1982+) for long-run analysis. No geographic breakdown.
- indhold is in 1.000 stk. (thousands of animals), not individual counts. Multiply by 1000 when combining with modern tables.
- husdyrtype has 31 values organized by animal species with "i alt" totals: 1035=Kvæg i alt, 1060=Får og geder i alt, 1080=Høns i alt, 1100=Gæs, ænder og kalkuner i alt, 1130=Svin i alt, 1175=Heste i alt. Use these i alt codes for broad species totals; subtypes like 1020=Køer, 1045=Moderfår, 1115=Søer provide breakdowns within species.
- No enhed selector — one row per husdyrtype×tid.
