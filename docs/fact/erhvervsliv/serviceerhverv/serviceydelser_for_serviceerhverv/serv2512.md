table: fact.serv2512
description: Omsætning for reklamevirksomhed efter medier og tid
measure: indhold (unit Pct.)
columns:
- medier: values [TOT1=Omsætning for reklamebureauvirksomhed i alt, 11600=Aviser, magasiner og tidsskrifter, 11700=Postomdelte reklamer, 11800=Radio, 11900=TV, 12000=Biografer, 12100=Internet, 12200=Udendørs reklamer, herunder på transportmidler, 12300=Andre]
- tid: date range 2023-01-01 to 2023-01-01
notes:
- Single snapshot: only 2023 data. No time-series analysis possible.
- Values are percentages (Pct.), not revenue amounts. The 8 media categories sum to exactly 100%. Do NOT sum indhold — each row is a share of total ad agency turnover.
- medier=TOT1 represents 100% (the total). It is redundant — use it only to confirm the sum.
- To compare advertising channels: SELECT medier, indhold FROM fact.serv2512 WHERE medier != 'TOT1'. Ranks channels by share.
- For absolute advertising revenue figures by service type, use serv2503 instead.
