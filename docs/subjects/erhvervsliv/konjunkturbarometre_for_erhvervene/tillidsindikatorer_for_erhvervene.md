notes:
- This subject has a single table: etillid (monthly, 1998–2025).
- etillid contains 5 indicators: the composite Erhvervstillidsindikator (TE) plus four sector indices for industri (KBI), bygge og anlæg (KBB), detailhandel (KBD), and serviceerhverv (KBS). Use TE for overall business confidence; use the sector codes for sector-level comparisons.
- The 5 indicators are independent series — never sum them. TE is already a composite and is not equal to the sum of the others.
- KBS only available from 2000-04-01; other indicators from 1998-01-01.
