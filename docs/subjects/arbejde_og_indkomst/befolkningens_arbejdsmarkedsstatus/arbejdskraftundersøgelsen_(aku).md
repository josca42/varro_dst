notes:
- Table naming convention: suffix k=quarterly (data to 2025), a=annual (data to 2024), m=monthly (aku111m only, data to 2025). Check the suffix to pick the right time granularity.
- Numbers vs. rates: even-numbered base tables (100/110/120/...) = counts in 1.000 persons. Odd-numbered (101/111/121/...) = percentages or rates. aku410a/aku420a = average hours (Timer). aku550k = counts but requires filtering enhed.

- Headline indicators (employment rate, unemployment rate, participation rate): aku101k (seasonally adjusted quarterly) or aku111m (monthly, compare adjusted vs unadjusted). For breakdown by age/gender use aku111k.
- Counts of employed/unemployed/outside-labour-force: aku100k (seasonal adjustment, no breakdown), aku110k (age × gender), aku120k (by region), aku130a (by education level, annual).
- Employment detail: aku210k (employment type: lønmodtager/selvstændig, quarterly), aku230k (region × age × gender), aku240k (normal working hour band distribution), aku250k (desired vs. actual hours), aku260a (full/part-time contract, annual), aku320a (contract length, annual).
- Industry breakdowns: aku220k (employed by industry × region × gender, quarterly), aku310k (overtime by industry), aku420a (average hours by industry, annual).
- Working hours: aku240k (distribution, quarterly), aku410a (average by age/gender, annual), aku420a (average by industry, annual). Always filter to one arbejdstid measurement type in aku410a/aku420a.
- Homeworking: aku280k (quarterly 2008-2025), aku280a (annual 2008-2024), laby35 (by municipality group, annual 2021-2024 only).
- Atypical hours: aku270a (evening/night/Saturday/Sunday, annual). atyp categories are independent — never sum across them.
- Unemployment composition: aku510k (AKU-ledige by registration status, quarterly), aku520k (registered unemployed × AKU labour market status), aku540a (detailed decomposition by availability and benefit type, annual).
- Labour reserve: aku550k (counts, filter enhed!) and aku560k (rates, filter enhed!) — broadest measure of underemployment.
- Outside labour force: aku610a (annual, by self-reported reason: pensioner/student/sick/etc.).
- aku100k vs aku110k: aku100k is seasonally adjusted but has no demographic breakdown. aku110k adds age and gender but is not seasonally adjusted.
- Map: aku120k, aku121k, aku220k, aku230k all have region-level omrade (niveau 1, 5 regioner) — choropleth via /geo/regioner.parquet, merge on omrade=dim_kode.
- All tables with alder, kon/koen, uddniv include TOT rows — always filter to avoid overcounting.
- aku550k/aku560k require filtering enhed (unit) before querying — mixing persons and FTEs, or different % denominators, gives wrong results.
