notes:
- **Survey tables (bebrit*, kv2*, fabrit01)**: All values are percentages (Pct.). A `type` or `kon`/`alder` total row always exists — filter to it (type='10', kon='10', alder='TOT') to get national figures. Never sum across the demographic dimensions.
- **Multiple-choice category columns**: Many category columns are NOT mutually exclusive (respondents tick all that apply). Never sum across them. Key non-exclusive columns: bebrit08 `produkt`, bebrit09 `formaal`, bebrit11 `forhand`, bebrit21 `adfaerd`, bebrit22 `overv`, kv2sm1 `some`, kv2sm2 `indtyp`, kv2sp1 `lokation`, kv2sp2 `genre`, kv2sp5 `medspil`, kv2sp9 `forbrug`, kv2ai2 `formaaal`. Mutually exclusive (single-answer) columns: bebrit02 `senanv`, bebrit07 `senkob`, kv2pc1/sm3/sm4/sp4/sp7/ai1 `hyp`.
- **bebrit series (2008-2025)**: Long annual time series for internet usage (bebrit02), online purchasing (bebrit07, bebrit08, bebrit11), internet purposes (bebrit09), and household access (fabrit01). Use for trends over time. Demographic breakdown via `type` column covering alder, køn, beskæftigelse, uddannelse, and indkomst.
- **bebrit21/22 (2024 only)**: IT equipment replacement behavior (bebrit21) and purchase considerations (bebrit22). Single-year snapshots.
- **kv2 series (2024 only)**: Detailed 2024 survey on podcasts (kv2pc1-2), social media (kv2sm1-4), digital gaming (kv2sp1-9), and AI usage (kv2ai1-2). Demographic breakdown via `kon` and `alder`. No time trends possible — single year.
- **Broadband infrastructure tables**: dis122 (subscription counts by technology type 2000-2024), dis129 (counts + market shares private vs. business 2002-2024), laby51 (% housing covered by fixed broadband by municipality group 2014-2024). These are register data, not surveys.
- **laby51**: Only table with a regional dimension (kommunegrupper). Filter dim niveau to 1 (5 groups) or 2 (98 kommuner) to avoid double-counting. National total is komgrp=0 (not in dim table).
- **dis129 trap**: `antalandel` is a measurement selector (Antal vs Markedsandel). Never mix or sum across these two values.
- **kv2sm1 SQL note**: The column `some` is a reserved word in PostgreSQL — always quote it: SELECT f."some" FROM fact.kv2sm1 f.
- Map: laby51 supports choropleth at kommune level — /geo/kommuner.parquet, merge on komgrp=dim_kode (filter dim.kommunegrupper niveau=2). Exclude komgrp=0. No other tables in this subject have geographic columns.
