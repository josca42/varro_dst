notes:
- **Macroeconomic significance (knatio1–3)**: knatio1 covers all cultural+creative industries combined, knatio2 breaks down by kulturemne (with a non-standard K-code classification), knatio3 breaks down by 11 kreative erhverv. All three share the same selector structure: always filter `aktp` (measure type: production/import/export/GVA/employment/FTE), `prisenhed` (V=current prices, Y=2020 chain-linked), and `effekter` (TOT3=total, DIR/IND=components) before aggregating.
- **Public cultural funding (bevil01b, bevil02, bevil04b, bevil05b)**: bevil02 is the long-series table (2007–2025) without `formal` breakdown. bevil01b goes from 2020 but adds `formal` (specific budget lines). bevil04b and bevil05b are subsets of bevil01b for education-related and research-related funding only. All bevil tables require special handling for the kulturemne join: codes 1–6 match dim.kulturemner niveau-1 directly; codes 7–34 require `'K'||LPAD(code::text, 2, '0')` to get the K-prefixed niveau-2 label. The `finans` column has hierarchical aggregates (405=all public, 404=all state) — pick one level.
- **Ministry disbursements (kubs series)**: kubs01 is the simplest (kulturemne+tid only, 2010–2023). kubs05/kubs06 are paired tables for personal recipients: Antal vs. 1.000 kr. kubs07/kubs08 are paired for virksomheder: Antal vs. 1.000 kr. kubs02b/kubs04b/kubs09b/kubs10b add geographic breakdown (omrade→dim.nuts) plus selector columns `enhed` and `statsinstituition` that MUST be filtered. kubs11 covers international disbursements with a `landtyp` selector (Sagsland vs. Modtagerland) that must be filtered.
- **omrade join gotcha (kubs02b, kubs04b, kubs09b, kubs10b)**: The documented text join fails for region and landsdel codes because of leading zeros in the fact table (e.g. '081' vs. dim kode 81). Use `f.omrade::int = d.kode` instead.
- **Gender equity indicators (ligeki1, ligeki2)**: Both use `indikator` with percentage values (LA1=Mænd%, LA2=Kvinder%, LA3=difference pp). Never sum across indicators. ligeki1 = recipient counts, ligeki2 = disbursement amounts.
- **Biblioteksafgift (bia01, bia02)**: bia01 = recipient count (Antal), bia02 = disbursement (1.000 kr.). Both break down by bopomr (dim.nuts), kon, and alder. The biblioteksafgift is a public lending right fee paid to authors.
- **laby40**: Average payment per recipient by kommunegruppe type (5 groups). Values are kr./modtager — do not sum across groups.
- **Map-ready tables**: kubs05, kubs06, kubs07, kubs08, bia01, bia02, ligeki1, ligeki2 support choropleth maps at kommune/landsdel/region level via bopomr or kubsbeli joined to /geo/. kubs02b, kubs04b, kubs09b, kubs10b also support maps via omrade (use int cast).
