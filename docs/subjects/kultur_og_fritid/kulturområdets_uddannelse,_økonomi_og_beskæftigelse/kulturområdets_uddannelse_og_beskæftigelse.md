notes:
## Kunstner-tabeller (kunst1–kunst9)
- kunst1–5: Kunstnertællinger (Antal) for 2022 ONLY. Each covers a different demographic breakdown: kunst1=køn+alder, kunst2=bopælsregion (dim.nuts niveau=1), kunst3=indkomstgrundlag, kunst4=familietype, kunst5=uddannelsesniveau.
- kunst6a/7a/8a: Kunstnerindkomst (2019–2023). CRITICAL: always filter both enhed (6 measurement types: mean/median/quartiles/count of income sources/person count) AND prisenhed (fixed vs nominal prices) — every row is repeated 12 times. kunst6a=by gender, kunst7a=by age, kunst8a=by indkomstgrundlag.
- kunst9: Income by uddannelsesniveau (2022 only, no prisenhed column).
- For artist counts over time: use enhed=3090 in kunst6a/7a/8a (2019–2023). kunst1–5 only have 2022.

## Kulturiværksætteri (kuiv1–kuiv2)
- kuiv1: By cultural topic (6 broad categories, 2008–2023). kuiv2: By landsdel (11 landsdele, dim.nuts niveau=2, 2008–2023).
- Both have enhed selector (30=Jobs, 50=Nye virksomheder) — always filter to one.

## Kulturens erhvervsstruktur (kuerh1–kuerh3)
- kuerh1: By kulturemne (detailed K-code categories) with 4-measure enhed selector (jobs/workplaces/FTEs/wages) — always filter enhed.
- kuerh2: By geography (mixed region+kommune, dim.nuts niveau=1 or 3) with 2-measure enhed selector. CRITICAL: filter d.niveau when joining nuts — the column mixes both levels.
- kuerh3: Workplace size distribution by kulturemne. No enhed selector; counts workplaces only.
- kulturemne numeric codes in kuerh/kuarb/kuiv tables do NOT join reliably to dim.kulturemner (K-prefix mismatch). Use inline column values.

## Kulturansatte (kuarb1–kuarb3)
- kuarb1: By uddannelse (KU=kunstnerisk / AU=anden). kuarb2: By alder+herkomst. kuarb3: By jobtype (HJ/BJ).
- All three: 2008–2023, kulturemne codes unreliable for dim join.
- kuarb3 WARNING: HJ and BJ are not mutually exclusive per person — do not sum for person counts.

## Dimittender fra kulturuddannelser (kubesk11–kubesk55)
- All kubesk tables cover 14 named cultural higher-education institutions (music conservatories, architecture/design/film schools). Time range: 2019–2024 depending on table.
- kubesk11: Unemployment rates (Pct.) by institution/education/cohort/gender. CRITICAL: ledtyp selector (netto vs brutto) — always filter.
- kubesk22: Employment rates (Pct.) by institution/education/cohort/gender. No ledtyp split.
- kubesk33: Employment counts (Antal) with sector breakdown.
- kubesk44: Mean income (Kr.) by institution/education/cohort/gender. CRITICAL: indkomsttype selector (Erhvervsindkomst vs Personindkomst) — always filter. Values are means, never SUM.
- kubesk55: Employment counts with regional breakdown (dim.nuts niveau=1, 5 regioner).

## Studerende på Kulturministeriets institutioner (kultudd–kultudd2)
- kultudd: Enrolled/completed/new-entrant counts with international breakdown. 2005–2024. CRITICAL: fstatus selector (B/F/T) — always filter.
- kultudd1: Dropout counts only (2005–2019, no recent data).
- kultudd2: Mean study duration in months (2005–2024). Values are averages, not sums.
- Note: kultudd covers 8 institutions (music/scene/film only), while kubesk covers 14 (adds architecture/design schools).

## Folkehøjskoler (fohoj01–fohoj04)
- ALL fohoj tables: CRITICAL double measurement selector — always filter BOTH enhed (11=Kursister headcount vs 13=Årselever FTE) AND tidspunkter (22=Skoleår vs 11=Kalenderår). Forgetting either gives 4× overcounting.
- fohoj01: All course types, demographic breakdown (køn/alder/herkomst). 2016–2024.
- fohoj02a/02b: Medium+long courses only. 02a=family education level, 02b=family equivalised income.
- fohoj03a/03b: Short courses only. 03a=individual education level, 03b=personal income.
- fohoj04: Geographic breakdown by landsdel (codes 1–11 = dim.nuts niveau=2) and institution type (folkehøjskoler/ungdomshøjskoler/seniorhøjskoler).
