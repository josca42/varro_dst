notes:
- Table naming convention: "nah" = national accounts annual, "nkh" = national accounts quarterly (kvartal). Within that: "l" suffix = "hoved" (main posts), "o" suffix = "oversigt" (overview/more detail), "d" suffix = "detaljeret" (most granular), number suffix = account section (2=produktion/BNP, 3=indkomst/forbrug, 4=udland).

- Annual vs quarterly: most topics have both annual (nah*) and quarterly (nkh*) versions. Quarterly tables always have a saeson column (N/Y) that doubles row counts — always filter it. Annual tables go further back in time (1966 vs 1990 for quarterly).

- The prisenhed selector is the most common overcounting trap: almost every table with "Mio. kr." has V=løbende priser and LAN/LKV=2020-priser (kædede værdier). Every row exists once per prisenhed — always filter to one value. Note that quarterly tables use LKV while annual tables use LAN for the chained price series.

- By topic/account section:
  - Varer og tjenester (supply/use): nah1 (annual 1966), nkh1 (quarterly 1990, has saeson+prisenhed)
  - Produktion, BNP og indkomstdannelse: nahl2 (hoved 1966), naho2 (oversigt 1995), nahd21+nahd22 (detaljeret 1995), nkho2 (quarterly 1990)
  - Indkomst, forbrug, opsparing: naho3 (oversigt 1966), nahd31+nahd32+nahd33+nahd34 (detaljeret by stage 1995), nkho3 (quarterly 1990)
  - Danmark og udlandet: nahl4 (hoved 1966), naho4 (oversigt 1995), nahd4 (detaljeret 1995), nkhl4 (quarterly 1990)

- For BNP questions: B1GQD=BNP in almost all transakt-based tables. nahl2/naho2 are the cleanest starting points; nahd21 has the fullest breakdown.

- Household consumption: choose by COICOP detail level — nahc021 (15 groups, 1966–2024), nahc022 (44 groups), nahc023 (73 groups, to 2022 only). By durability: nahc3. By consumer type (husholdninger/NPISH/offentlige): nahc1 or nahc011. Quarterly: nkhc021, nkhc3, nkhc1.

- Distributional tables: naht1 (by income quintile) and naht2 (by household type) are unique — they have a tal selector (mio.kr. / per household / count) and only cover 2018–2022.

- Investment and capital: nahi (bruttoinvesteringer by asset type, 1966), nahk (full capital account incl. stocks, revaluations), nkhi (quarterly investment). nahk has the most complex structure (behold selector + aktiv hierarchy + prisenhed).

- Financial accounts: nahfk (annual 1995) and nkhfk (quarterly 1999) — three selectors (konto1, balance, finins) must all be filtered. konto1='T' for transaction flows.

- Employment: nahb (annual 1966, simple 3-value socio), nkhb (quarterly with saeson).

- vnahc021 is a version-comparison table for revisions to nahc021 — use nahc021 for analysis, vnahc021 only when studying revisions.

- nahc12 (kollektiv forbrug by COFOG function): the dim.cofog join is broken — funktion uses CO01-CO10 codes not numeric. COT=total, CO01-CO10=10 COFOG main groups.
