notes:
- Naming conventions: tables ending in 'k' are quarterly (kvartal); tables starting with 'v' are version/revision tables (alternative historical estimates); tables starting with 'n' use national accounts format (with prisenhed selector for price bases).
- All quarterly 'k' tables have tid as quarterly dates (1999-01-01=Q1, 1999-04-01=Q2, etc.); all tables with saeson (off10k, off12k, nkof1) require saeson filtering (N/Y) to avoid doubling values.

Broad public sector income/expenditure:
- off3 (annual 1971–2024, with sektor S/F/K+R/R/K) or off3k (quarterly 1999–2025, no sektor) for the full expenditure/revenue account. voff3 for revision comparisons. Key rows: ui=2.19 for the public balance (saldo).
- off26 / off26k: narrower focus on forbrugsudgifter (consumption expenditure) only, with the individuel/kollektiv split.

COFOG functional breakdown:
- off29: total expenditure by COFOG function (77 categories, 2 levels). Annual 1995–2024. Note dotted notation mismatch with dim.cofog — see off29 notes for join SQL.
- off24: forbrugsudgift by COFOG niveau=1 (10 groups) × individuelt/kollektivt. konsumgrp is a measurement selector — must filter to avoid 3× overcounting.
- off25: cross-tabulation of COFOG function × expenditure type (udtype). Annual 1998–2024.
- nof2: forbrugsudgift by COFOG function × forbrugsart × prisenhed. CO-prefixed funktion codes need REPLACE for dim join. Annual 2015–2024.
- nof1 / nkof1: forbrugsudgifter in national accounts format with price bases (løbende/2020-priser). nkof1 is quarterly (1999–2025) with saeson selector.

Transfers and subsidies:
- off10 / off10k: indkomstoverførsler til husholdninger. off10k is quarterly with saeson selector.
- off17: subsidier til virksomheder (1971–2024). Hierarchical tilskudsart — I=Danish-financed total, II=EU programs, III=Danish programs.
- off18: renter (renteudgifter and renteindtægter). Annual 1980–2024.

Taxes:
- off12 (annual 1971–2024), off12k (quarterly 1999–2025 with saeson), voff12 (revision comparisons). 47 hierarchical skatter categories.

Financial accounts (balance-sheet):
- off13: finansielle konti with sektor breakdown (S13/S1311/S1313/S1314). Annual 1995–2024. sektor join to dim.esa requires dot-insertion fix. Multiple required filters: konto, aktpas, konsol, modseknat.
- off22: same financial accounts without sektor split. Quarterly 1995–2025. Use off22 for high-frequency; off13 for sektor detail.

State finances and debt (Nationalbanken data):
- dnsosb: beholdninger (stocks), monthly 1990–2025. Unit: Mia. kr. 2000.0=Bruttogæld, 3000.0=Statsgæld.
- dnsost: transaktioner (flows), monthly 1987–2025. Unit: Mia. kr. 1000=Bruttofinansieringsbehov.

Public sector institutional accounts:
- off14: full institutional accounts (sektor 1=all public, 11–13=sub-sectors, 2=public enterprises, 21–27=branches). Annual 1993–2023.
- off14a: public enterprises only (sektor 2, 21–27). Annual 1993–2023.

R&D budgets:
- foubud: aggregate public R&D budget (2001–2025). prisenhed selector (AARPRIS/08PRIS required). Warning: bevilling 8000 and 9000 are % of BNP, not Mio. kr.
- foubud1: detailed by forskningsformål × sektor × bevillingstype. foubud4: sektor only. foubud5: formål only. All extend to 2028 (future years are budget forecasts, not actuals). bevilling1=1 always equals 2+3 — don't double-count.
