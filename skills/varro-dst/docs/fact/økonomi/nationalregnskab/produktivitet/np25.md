table: fact.np25
description: Produktivitetsudviklingen efter branche, type, prisenhed og tid
measure: indhold (unit Pct.)
columns:
- branche: join dim.nr_branche on branche=kode [approx]
- type: values [LP=Arbejdsproduktivitet, IT=It-kapitalintensitet, AK=Anden kapitalintensitet, LQ=Uddannelsesniveau, TF=Totalfaktorproduktivitet]
- prisenhed: values [VPR_V=Årlig vækstrate i pct., VPR_G=Årlig vækstrate i pct., rullende 5-års gennemsnit]
- tid: date range 1967-01-01 to 2023-01-01
dim docs: /dim/nr_branche.md

notes:
- branche uses V-prefixed codes that do NOT directly match dim.nr_branche. Correct join: JOIN dim.nr_branche d ON d.kode = REPLACE(SUBSTRING(f.branche, 2), '_', '-'). Handles letter codes (VA→A), combined codes (VD_E→D-E, VG_I→G-I, VM_N→M-N, VR_S→R-S), and numeric codes (V01000→01000).
- branche niveaux 1–4 are all present simultaneously. Filter WHERE d.niveau = N to pick one level and avoid double-counting.
- Three productivity-specific aggregate codes sit outside dim.nr_branche: PBY (erhverv/private business), PMIALT (markedsbestemt erhverv/market economy), PTJEN (tjenester/services). No PIALT (total economy) here — see np23 for that.
- prisenhed is a measurement selector — always filter to one value: VPR_V (annual growth rate pct.) or VPR_G (rolling 5-year average). Forgetting this doubles row counts.
- type selects the productivity decomposition component: LP=Arbejdsproduktivitet (total), IT=It-kapitalintensitet, AK=Anden kapitalintensitet, LQ=Uddannelsesniveau, TF=Totalfaktorproduktivitet. These are decomposition contributions — do NOT sum across types. Filter to type='LP' for headline labour productivity growth.
- For headline annual labour productivity growth by sector: WHERE prisenhed='VPR_V' AND type='LP'. For long-run smoothed trend: prisenhed='VPR_G'.
