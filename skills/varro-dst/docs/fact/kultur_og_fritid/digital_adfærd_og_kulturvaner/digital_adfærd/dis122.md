table: fact.dis122
description: Bredbåndsabonnementer efter bredbåndstype og tid
measure: indhold (unit Antal)
columns:
- bredbaand: values [160750=I alt, 160701=xDSL, 160705=Kabelmodem, 160710=Fiber til private, 160712=FTTx, 160713=Fast trådløst, 160715=Satellit, 160720=Powerline Communications, 160725=WLL, 160730=WiFi, 160735=WiMAX, 160740=LAN, 160745=Øvrige]
- tid: date range 2000-07-01 to 2024-07-01
notes:
- bredbaand='160750' is I alt (national total). The other values are individual broadband technology types. Unlike survey tables, dis122 is a count-based register: I alt equals the sum of individual types. Filter to bredbaand != '160750' to aggregate by technology, or use '160750' for the overall count.
- Long time series: July 2000 to July 2024, with mid-year (July) observations. tid values are '2000-07-01', '2001-07-01', etc.
- Values are counts of subscriptions (Antal), not percentages.
