table: fact.bdf107
description: Bedrifter efter område, type, alder og tid
measure: indhold (unit -)
columns:
- omrade: join dim.nuts on omrade=kode [approx]; levels [1, 2]
- type: values [ANTAL=Bedrifter (antal), AREAL=Samlet dyrket areal (ha)]
- alder: values [IALT=Alder i alt, 25UN=Under 25 år, 25-29=25-29 år, 30-34=30-34 år, 35-39=35-39 år, 40-44=40-44 år, 45-49=45-49 år, 50-54=50-54 år, 55-59=55-59 år, 60-64=60-64 år, 65-69=65-69 år, 70-74=70-74 år, 75OV=75 år og derover, 999=Uoplyst alder]
- tid: date range 2006-01-01 to 2024-01-01
dim docs: /dim/nuts.md

notes:
- omrade joins dim.nuts with the same 12 codes as bdf11: niveau 1 (81-85 = regioner), niveau 2 (4,7,8,9,10 = 5 landsdele), plus aggregates 0 (Hele landet) and 15 (unmatched, likely Sjælland og øerne). See bdf11 notes for join details.
- type is a measure-type selector: ANTAL (bedrifter count) or AREAL (ha). Filter to one — never sum across both.
- alder gives farmer age distribution in 5-year bands. IALT = all ages. 999=Uoplyst alder is a real category, not a total. Filter to IALT when no age breakdown needed.
- To get national farm count by farmer age: WHERE type='ANTAL' AND omrade=0 AND GROUP BY alder (excluding IALT to avoid double-count).
- Map: /geo/regioner.parquet (niveau 1, omrade 81-85) or /geo/landsdele.parquet (niveau 2, omrade 4,7,8,9,10 — 5 of 11 landsdele only) — merge on omrade=dim_kode. Exclude omrade IN (0, 15).
