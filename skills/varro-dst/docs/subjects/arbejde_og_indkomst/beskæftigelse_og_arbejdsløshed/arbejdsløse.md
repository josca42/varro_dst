## Table families

This subject has several parallel families covering fuldtidsledige (full-time equivalent unemployed). Pick by series type first, then by which breakdown you need.

**AUL/AULK family — counts, full breakdown (omrade+ydelse+akasse+alder+kon):**
- aulk01: current (to 2025-04), from 2007, finer alder bands (5-year groups). Preferred for current data.
- auf01: preliminary (foreløbig, to 2025-09), from Jul 2017 only, broader alder bands (10-year groups). Use for the most up-to-date figures.
- auf02: same structure as auf01 but from 2007, slightly less recent than auf01.
- aul01: superseded by aulk01 (stopped 2024-01). Use aulk01 instead.

**AUP/AULKP family — unemployment rate (pct. of workforce):**
- aulkp01: current (to 2025-04), from 2007, finer alder bands. Preferred for current rates by region/age/gender. Note: unit annotation says "Antal" but values are percentages.
- aup01: preliminary (foreløbig, to 2025-09), from Jul 2017, broader alder bands.
- aup02: from 2007, slightly less recent than aup01, broader alder bands.
- aup03: pct. of insured (not workforce — different denominator), foreløbig, has akasse breakdown.
- aulp01: superseded by aulkp01 (stopped 2024-01).

**AUS family — seasonally adjusted, national:**
- aus09: ledighedsindikator, national, counts and rates for bruttoledige/dagpenge/kontanthjælp.
- aus07: counts+rates broken down by ydelsestype, køn, AND alder all in one yd column (unusual structure).
- aus08: regional breakdown with seasonal adjustment only (no demographic breakdown).
- aus05: special series for kontanthjælpsmodtagere (ikke arbejdsmarkedsparate) + feriedagpengemodtagere.

**AULK02/03/04 — specialized breakdowns:**
- aulk02: fuldtidsledige + ledighedsberørte by ledgrad (degree of unemployment).
- aulk03: counts by origin country (ieland). Current (to 2025-04).
- aulk04: langtidsledige (long-term, ≥26 weeks) by region/age/gender.
- aulk07/08: langtidsledige forsikringsaktive by a-kasse (07=by kon, 08=by alder).
- aulk09/10: ledighedsforløb (spell durations) by cumulative thresholds.

**Historical:**
- aulaar: longest series (from 1979), netto (dagpenge only), national total only.

**Workforce denominators (RAS):**
- ras400: workforce by region/age/gender (annual, ultimo november, stopped 2023).
- rasie11: workforce by origin country/age/gender.
- rasie22: workforce by origin country/region.

**Gender equality indicators (LIGEAI):**
- ligeai9a: annual, by region and age.
- ligeai9b: monthly seasonally adjusted, national only. Most current.
- ligeai10: monthly, for long-term unemployed.

**Insurance-active members:**
- aua01: forsikringsaktive by region/a-kasse/age/gender. Denominator for rates. From 2000.

## Common pitfalls

- **Measurement selectors**: saesonfak (aus-tables), saeson (aus05), enhed (aulk04/07/08/09/10), perpct (aul08/aulaar), maengde4 (aulk07/08) all double/quadruple row counts. Always filter to exactly one value before aggregating.
- **omrade='0'**: All tables with omrade include code 0=Hele landet, which is NOT in dim.nuts. Use WHERE f.omrade = '0' for national total; join dim.nuts for regional breakdown. Also 997=Udlandet and 998=Uoplyst exist in some tables (aua01, auf01).
- **ieland does NOT join dim.lande_uhv**: Tables aul03, aulk03, aulp03, rasie11, rasie22 use a custom 39-code classification. Use ColumnValues to see codes; do not join dim.lande_uhv.
- **hfudd does NOT join dim.ddu_udd**: aul08 uses H-prefix education codes. Use ColumnValues("aul08","hfudd").
- **Rate tables**: All aup/aulp/aulkp tables contain percentages — do not sum across regions or age groups.
- **kmdr in aulk09/10**: Cumulative duration thresholds — never sum across kmdr values.
- **ydelsestype**: TOT=Bruttoledige is the aggregate of LDP+LKT+ADP+AKT. Filter to one.
- **aus07 yd column**: Unusually mixes ydelsestype, køn, and alder in one column. Select exactly one yd value.

## Map support

Tables with `omrade` support choropleth maps via /geo/. Merge on omrade=dim_kode; exclude omrade=0.
- niveau 1 (5 regioner) and niveau 3 (98 kommuner): auf01, auf02, aup01, aup02, aup03, aulk01, aulk04, aulkp01, aulp01, aul01, aua01, ras400, aus08, ligeai9a, ligeai10
- niveau 2 (11 landsdele) only: aulk03, aul03, aul08, aulp03, rasie22
