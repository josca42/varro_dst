notes:
- **Visiteret vs leveret**: Many tables come in parallel pairs — visiteret (authorized/allocated) vs leveret (actually delivered). Visiteret: aed06/aed022/aed021/aed12/aed07/aed14. Leveret: aed023/aed01/aed02/aed012. Visiteret goes back to 2008; leveret from 2011-2013. Use visiteret unless the question is specifically about delivered care.
- **Person counts vs hours vs pct**: aed06/aed023/aed07 count persons. aed022/aed01 are total hours per week. aed021/aed02 are average hours per person per week (don't sum). aed12/aed012/aed21 are percentages (don't sum). Pick the right measure for the question.
- **Home (eget hjem) vs care home (plejehjem/plejebolig)**: aed06/aed022/aed021/aed12/aed14/aed01/aed02/aed023/aed012 all cover hjemmehjælp in own home. aed07 covers persons in care homes receiving home help. For the care-home population use aed07 (persons) or laby20 (pct).
- **Regional breakdown**: Most tables have omrade joining dim.nuts at niveau 1 (5 regioner) and niveau 3 (98 kommuner). Some are niveau 3 only: aed21, vh33, aed08, rehab19, aed16. omrade=0 is always the national total (not in dim — use directly without JOIN). Always filter WHERE d.niveau=1 OR d.niveau=3, not both at once.
- **ydelsestype totals**: All tables with ydelsestype have a total row (100 or 450/50). The sub-categories (200, 300, 400 for care type; or 500, 550, 600... for hour-type) overlap or nest. Always filter to the total code (100 or 450/50) unless specifically breaking down by care type.
- **timeuge selector in aed06/aed023**: These tables also have a timeuge column (hour bands). Always filter timeuge=50 (i alt) unless showing the hour-band distribution — failing to filter inflates counts 7-fold.
- **Specialized tables**: aed21 = quick service-level indicators by kommune (pct. of 67+ receiving each service type). aed10a = preventive home visits (from 2016). vh33 = count of private providers per kommune. aed16 = waiting lists and wait times for care homes (5 different metrics in maengde4 — always filter to one). aed08 = genoptræning/vedligeholdelsestræning. rehab19 = rehabilitering (from 2019, niveau 3 only, column is alder1 not alder).
- **Gender equality tables**: ligehi12/ligehi13 compare men vs women in home help by family type. paaroe50/paaroe51 compare home help rates/hours by family-relation situation (partner/pårørende). All limited to 65-84 age range. laby20 breaks down by kommunegruppe type (only from 2022).
- **Private provider tables**: aed12 (visiteret pct.), aed012 (leveret pct.), aed14 (number switching providers), vh33 (number of approved providers) all relate to private home-help delivery.
- **Maps**: All tables except laby20 support choropleth maps. Niveau 1+3 tables (aed01/02/012/021/022/023/06/07/10a/12/14/ligehi12/ligehi13/paaroe50/paaroe51): use /geo/kommuner.parquet or /geo/regioner.parquet — merge on omrade=dim_kode. Niveau 3 only (aed08/aed16/aed21/rehab19/vh33): use /geo/kommuner.parquet. laby20 uses kommunegrupper (no geo boundary file).
