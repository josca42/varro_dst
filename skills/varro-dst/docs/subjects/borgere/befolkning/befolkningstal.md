## Table selection guide

**Population snapshots (headcount)**
- National + region + municipality, quarterly, back to 2008, with civilstand: **folk1a**
- National + region + municipality, monthly, from Oct 2021, no civilstand: **folk1am**
- National only, annual, back to 1971, with civilstand: **befolk1**
- National only, annual, back to 1901, 5-year age groups, no civilstand: **befolk2**
- Census-based, back to 1769, only 8 coarse geographic units: **ft** (do NOT join dim.nuts — incompatible codes)

**Sub-municipality and special geographies**
- Urban areas / landdistrikter, age+sex, annual from 2010: **by1**
- Municipality × bystørrelse × age+sex, annual from 2010: **by2**
- Urban areas, folketal+areal+befolkningstæthed (measurement selector!), from 2017: **by3**
- Postal codes, age+sex, 5-year age groups, annual from 2010: **postnr1** (unique codes) / **postnr2** (municipality-postal combos)
- Parishes, age+sex, annual from 2010: **sogn1**; quarterly with Folkekirke membership (no age/sex): **km1**
- Islands, annual from 1901: **bef4**

**Population movement (births, deaths, migration)**
- Municipality level, quarterly preliminary: **bev22**; annual final: **bev107**
- Parish level, annual from 2015: **kmsta003**
- Kommunegruppe rates (per 1,000 indb.), annual from 2007: **laby01**
- Long national trend (1901–) with mixed units: **hisb3** (WARNING: M+K/M/K are in thousands, all other codes are absolute counts)

**Average age**
- Region + municipality, annual from 2005: **galder** — indhold is a mean, never SUM
- Parish level, annual from 2007: **kmgalder** — indhold is a mean, never SUM

**Birth country / origin**
- By birth country (200+ countries), age+sex, from 1990: **bef5**
- Birthday/month/year distribution, from 2008: **folk3** (birth year), **folk3fod** (birth country DANSK/UDLAND only)
- Faroese-born in Denmark by parental origin: **bef5f**; Greenlandic-born: **bef5g**

**Map-ready tables**
- Kommune/region choropleth: **folk1a**, **folk1am** (omrade, niveau 1+3), **bev22**, **bev107** (omrade, niveau 3 only), **galder**, **by2** (komk, niveau 1+3 / niveau 3) — all merge on column=dim_kode via /geo/kommuner.parquet or regioner.parquet.
- Postal code choropleth: **postnr1** (pnr20=dim_kode, exclude 1+9999), **postnr2** (pnr20 % 10000 = dim_kode).
- Parish (sogn) choropleth: **km1**, **sogn1**, **kmsta003**, **kmgalder** — merge on sogn=dim_kode via /geo/sogne.parquet, exclude 0+9999.

**Urban distribution percentages**
- Age profile within each kommunegruppe: **laby02**
- Geographic distribution of each age group: **laby03**
- Residential stability (same group as 20 years ago): **laby05**

## Common pitfalls across this subject

- **folk1a / folk1am geographic overcounting**: omrade includes national (omrade='0'), 5 regions (niveau=1), and 99 municipalities (niveau=3) — each person counted 3× if you don't filter to one level. Verify: unfiltered SUM = 3× correct value.
- **All tables with kon, alder, civilstand have total rows**: TOT/IALT for non-target dimensions always needed; forgetting any one inflates the count.
- **Movement tables (bev22, bev107, kmsta003, laby01)**: bevaegelse codes are not independent — B04, B07, B10 sum to B11 (Befolkningstilvækst). Never sum all codes.
- **by3 folkartaet**: measurement selector mixing persons/km²/persons-per-km² in one column. Always filter to one folkartaet.
- **galder / kmgalder**: means, not counts. Never SUM; use weighted average if aggregating.
- **laby01/02/03/05**: rates/percentages. Never sum across geographic dimension.
