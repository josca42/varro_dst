notes:
## Population stock (1. januar / quarterly)
- **folk1e** — quarterly herkomst by omrade/kon/alder (west/non-west split). Current (to 2025). Simplest for "how many indvandrere/efterkommere by region?"
- **folk1b** — quarterly statsb by omrade/kon/alder with full country breakdown (~208 nationalities). Current. Use when you need specific nationalities.
- **folk1d** — quarterly statsb simplified (Dansk/Udenlandsk). Current. Simpler than folk1b but has individual-year alder.
- **folk1c** — quarterly herkomst + ieland (origin country) by omrade/kon/alder. ENDS 2020. The only table combining herkomst category AND origin country at quarterly/regional level.
- **folk2** — annual herkomst + statsb + ieland by alder/kon from 1980. No total rows (must SUM across kon/herkomst/statsb). Best for long historical series by origin country.
- **eb4** — annual population including "børn af efterkommere" (BAE) as separate herkomst category by kon/alder/ieland/omrade (kommuner only). Use when the 3rd-generation BAE distinction matters.

## Børn af efterkommere (3rd generation)
- **eb1** — country-level ieland, 5-year age bands, no regional breakdown. Most granular on origin.
- **eb2** — simplified ieland (vest/ikke-vest), individual-year alder, no regional breakdown.
- **eb3** — simplified ieland (vest/ikke-vest), 5-year age bands, by kommune. Use for regional distribution of this group.

## Percentage share
- **iepct** — annual share of total population by herkomst group (1980–2025). Pre-computed percentages — read-only, never sum.

## Naturalizations
- **dkstat** — annual count of people who gained Danish citizenship, by previous nationality/kon/alder (1979–2024). No total rows — must SUM.

## Permit type breakdown (FIRST vs CURRENT permit)
- indoph1/indoph3 = immigrants by first/current permit type × region (kommuner+regioner). indoph1=first permit, indoph3=current permit.
- indoph2/indoph4 = same but by origin country group instead of region. Prefer indoph4 (fully inline) over indoph2 (has a missing code 364 in dim.lande_psd).

## Asylum applications (flow)
- **van5m** — monthly asylum applications by nationality + asyltype. CRITICAL: always filter asyltype to BRU or SPO — do not sum across them. No total statsb.
- **van5rkam** — monthly registreringstallet (SPO) with gender/age breakdown by nationality.

## Residence permits granted (flow — not a population stock)
- Naming convention: van66=annual, van77=monthly; suffix p=permanent only, ka/kam=with gender+age.
- **van66** — annual all-permit grants by statsb+ophold (1997–2024). Longest historical series.
- **van66ka** — annual + gender/age (2014–2024).
- **van66p** — annual permanent permits only (2003–2024).
- **van77m** — monthly all-permit grants (2014–2025). Most current.
- **van77kam** — monthly + gender/age (2014–2025). Most granular.
- **van77pm** — monthly permanent permits only (2015–2025).
- All van66/van77 tables: statsb and ophold have no total codes — always SUM for aggregates.

## Immigration flows (people arriving)
- **van8a** — annual immigration flows by statsb/koen/ophold (1997–2024). Note: column is koen not kon.
- **van8k** — quarterly preliminary immigration flows (2016–2025). Same structure as van8a.
- These count NEW arrivals; van66/van77 count NEW permits granted (related but not identical).

## Cross-tabulations by parent background
- **kryds1** — all herkomst groups × person+parent country/citizenship (coarse V/IV/DK coding). 2019–2025.
- **kryds2** — dansk oprindelse only × parent country/citizenship (full country codes) × age. 2019–2025.
- **kryds3** — all herkomst groups × person+mother+father birth country (full country codes). 2019–2025.
- **kryds4** — indvandrere/efterkommere (west/non-west) × parent country/citizenship × age. 2019–2025.

## Parish level
- **kmsta001** — population by parish (søgn) × herkomst × church membership. Unique sub-municipality geography. herkomst column uses west/non-west coding, not dim.herkomst — treat as inline.

## Map support
- Regional/municipal choropleth: folk1b, folk1c, folk1d, folk1e, indoph1, indoph3, eb3, eb4 — all join via omrade=dim_kode to /geo/kommuner.parquet (niveau 3) or /geo/regioner.parquet (niveau 1). Exclude omrade=0 for tables that include a national total.
- Parish-level choropleth: kmsta001 — join via sogn=dim_kode to /geo/sogne.parquet. Exclude sogn IN (0, 9999).
- No map: folk2, dkstat, iepct, kryds1-4, eb1, eb2, indoph2, indoph4, van5m, van5rkam, van66*, van77*, van8*.

## Common pitfalls across all tables
- Most tables have omrade=0 as national total — this is NOT in dim.nuts (use for filtering only, don't join).
- statsb/ieland=0 in lande_psd-joined columns = "I alt" aggregate, not the dim.lande_psd "Helt uoplyst" label.
- Many tables lack total rows for some dimensions (especially folk2, dkstat, eb1/2/3, all van tables). Always check whether a TOT/IALT code exists before assuming SUM is needed.
