notes:
- **Motor vehicles (bil):** bil54 is the most flexible — monthly data from 2018 with regional breakdown (dim.nuts), vehicle type, user sector (husholdning/erhverv), and fuel type. bil707 is annual (2007–2025) with regional breakdown but no fuel/sector split. bil710 is annual (2018–2025) with region, ownership, and fuel — the go-to for EV analysis by geography.
- **Personbiler detail tables (no region):** bil8 (by age+type, annual from 1993), bil10 (by fuel+weight), bil11 (by usage+weight), bil21 (by age+fuel, from 2011), bil52 (by ownership+fuel, monthly from 2011). bil8 contains a special alder1='620' row (average age) that must be excluded when computing totals.
- **bil40 is a projection table** (fremskrivning) for personbilbestand 2025–2029, not historical data. Its bestanddel column mixes absolute counts and percentages — always filter to one bestanddel.
- **Other road vehicles:** bil15–bil17 (varebiler by fuel/weight/ownership/usage), bil18 (lastbiler by fuel/axles/weight), bil907 (påhængsvogne by region+weight/ownership), bil12–bil14 (busser by fuel/weight/ownership/usage/seats).
- **Railway (bane):** bane51 counts train units; bane52 covers capacity (seats and load in thousands). Use bane52's enhed column to distinguish: 3000=siddepladser, 3010=lasteevne.
- **Ships (skib):** skib11 (all ships by type+register+unit, from 1990) and skib14 (merchant ships only, detailed skibstype, from 1990) are the broadest. skib12 (by type+tonnage) and skib13 (by type+age) add size/age breakdowns. skib15–skib17 cover ships in foreign registers only (from 2017). All skib tables have an enhed selector (antal vs BT) — always filter to one.
- **Aircraft (flyv):** flyv11 only — aircraft count and seat capacity by aircraft type, from 1990. maengde4 selector: 155=antal fly, 158=pladser.
- **Shared gotcha (enhed/maengde4):** skib11–skib17, bane52, bil14, and flyv11 all have a unit-selector column. Every dimension combination appears once per unit type. Forgetting to filter silently doubles every value.
- **Regional joins (dim.nuts):** bil54, bil707, bil710, bil907 all join on omrade. omrade=0 (Hele landet) and omrade=99 (Uoplyst) are aggregate codes not in dim.nuts — use them directly without joining. niveau 1=5 regioner, 2=11 landsdele, 3=99 kommuner.
- **Map:** bil54, bil707, bil710, bil907 all support choropleth maps — /geo/kommuner.parquet (niveau 3), /geo/landsdele.parquet (niveau 2), or /geo/regioner.parquet (niveau 1) — merge on omrade=dim_kode.
